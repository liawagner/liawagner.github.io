Sunset at Weverton Cliffs

July 2, 2013

I don’t often get a chance to see a sunset quite as stunning as this one. Just around the corner from Harper’s Ferry, and yet it feels so isolated. By nightfall the cars on the highway nearby have dulled in sound and you can hear the wildlife taking precedence.

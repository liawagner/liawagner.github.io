Globe misleading new Fiber subscribers about 30% Throttle after reaching Data Cap!

Globe Telecom is misleading new Home Broadband Fiber subscribers about the 30% throttle after reaching their 'Data Cap', which should have been 6 Mbps for us. In reality they throttle to an unusable 400 kbps.

September 12, 2017 Christian

We subscribed to Globe Fiber in Davao City on July 21, 2017. The plan is advertised as follows: Broadband Plan 1599 Go Fast 20Mbps, 150GB UP TO 20MBPS BROWSING SPEED 150GB DATA ALLOCATION https://shop.globe.com.ph/products/broadband/plan-1599-go-fast At multiple occasions we inquired about what happens after we consume the 150 GB? Globe office in SM Davao City, as well as other Globe sales people trying to sell the contracts door-to-door in Matina, informed us in July before signing the contract, that we would be throttled to 30% of 20 Mbps!
